The first Docker images for the `PaddleX <https://github.com/PaddlePaddle/PaddleX>`__ framework are now available:

`https://github.com/waikato-datamining/paddlex <https://github.com/waikato-datamining/paddlex>`__

According to the project's website:

*PaddleX 3.0 is a low-code development tool for AI models built on the PaddlePaddle framework. It integrates
numerous ready-to-use pre-trained models, enabling full-process development from model training to inference,
supporting a variety of mainstream hardware both domestic and international, and aiding AI developers in industrial
practice.*
